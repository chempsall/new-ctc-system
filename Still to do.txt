Post-alpha:
There are a few types of RTC which will never link to Horizon but should not be treated as missing the link.
1 - Annual leave - a list of all employees in a department, sorted alphabetically, blank allocation on first run for people to enter their own holidays. Updated automatically to include all new members of staff in the department.
2 - Public holidays - Updated nightly from https://www.gov.uk/bank-holidays. Automatically populated UNLESS the value differs (is lower) than that expected (this will be because the staff member has chosen to work the public holiday and is taking the day elsewhere). Updated automatically to include all new members of staff in the department.
3 - Day Release and Study Leave. Updated automatically to include all members of staff whose grade is either P0 - Undergraduate Engineer/Consultant or P1 - Graduate/Assistant Engineer/Consultant. Updated automatically to include all new members of staff in the department who match this criteria. May contain other names which will be added manually.
4 - Training - a list of all employees in a department, sorted alphabetically, blank allocation on first run for people to enter their own holidays. Updated automatically to include all new members of staff in the department.

Staff leavers on RTCs — when a staff member leaves, future allocations should be automatically transferred to their generic grade equivalent and they should be removed from RTCs. Past allocations remain unchanged for audit purposes.
